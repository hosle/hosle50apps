Features

Everything You Need

Simple, fast, and reliable traffic monitoring for your daily commute or weekend trips.

Real-Time Updates

Camera feeds refresh automatically every 5 minutes, showing you the latest traffic conditions at the checkpoint.

Multiple Views

Switch between page view for detailed images or list view to quickly scan all camera feeds at once.

Multiple Cameras

View all checkpoint cameras including approaches from both Singapore and Johor sides of the causeway.

Timestamp Info

Each image shows exactly when it was captured, so you know how current the traffic situation is.

Lightweight

Minimal app size and low data usage. Perfect for quick checks before heading out.

Cross Platform

Available on both iOS and Android. Same great experience on any device.

About

The Busiest Land Crossing

Woodlands Checkpoint handles over 300,000 travelers daily, making it one of the busiest land border crossings in the world.

Plan Smarter, Travel Better

Whether you're commuting daily between Singapore and Johor Bahru, or planning a weekend getaway to Malaysia, checking the traffic before you leave can save you hours of waiting.

Our app pulls live camera feeds directly from LTA (Land Transport Authority), giving you accurate, real-time information about traffic conditions at the causeway.
